A few facts about the world we live in and other random facts

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and is the largest of the terrestial planets. Unlike any other planets in the solar system that are named after the classic deities, the Earth's name comes from the Anglo-Saxon word erda which means ground or soil. The Earth was formed approximately 4.45 billion years ago and is the only known planet to support life.

Top 3 earth facts

  2. A year isn't exactly 365 days, and the earth doesn't take 24 hours to rotate
